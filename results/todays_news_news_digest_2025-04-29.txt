The European Union has introduced new regulations to curb the growing power of tech giants like Google, Amazon, and Facebook. The rules aim to promote fair competition, protect user data, and prevent monopolies. Tech companies will need to comply with these regulations or face hefty fines.

A severe heatwave has gripped India, resulting in the deaths of hundreds of people. The extreme temperatures have also led to water shortages, crop damage, and widespread disruption to daily life. The Indian government has issued heatwave alerts and is working to provide relief to affected areas.

The bill aims to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, invest in renewable energy, and promote sustainable infrastructure development.
