Job Description: Content Writer
Role: -
We are seeking a creative and detail-oriented Content Writer to join our team at Elluminati. The
ideal candidate will have a strong passion for writing, a deep understanding of content strategy,
and the ability to create compelling and engaging content across various platforms. This role is
crucial in helping us communicate our clients’ brand stories effectively and resonate with their
target audiences.
Responsibilities: -
• Content Creation: Develop high-quality, engaging, and original content for various digital
platforms, including websites, blogs, social media, email campaigns, and more.
• Content Strategy: Collaborate with the marketing team to create content strategies that align
with client objectives and enhance their brand presence.
• Research: Conduct thorough research on industry-related topics, including keyword
research, to produce content that is both informative and SEO-friendly.
• Editing and Proofreading: Review and edit content to ensure it is free of errors, consistent in
style, and adheres to brand guidelines.
• SEO Optimization: Implement SEO best practices in content creation to improve organic
search rankings and drive traffic to client websites.
• Content Management: Manage and maintain content calendars, ensuring timely delivery of
content for all projects.
• Collaboration: Work closely with designers, developers, and other marketing team members
to create cohesive and effective campaigns.
• Content Performance Analysis: Monitor and analyse content performance metrics, making
recommendations for improvements based on data-driven insights.
• Client Communication: Engage with clients to understand their content needs and provide
solutions that align with their marketing goals.
Skills Required: -
• Proven experience in web content writing, content strategy, and digital content creation.
• Strong writing, editing, and proofreading skills, with a keen eye for detail.
• Ability to conduct in-depth research and translate complex topics into clear, engaging
content.
• Familiarity with SEO principles and the ability to implement them in content creation.
• Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
• Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced team environment.
• Strong organizational skills and the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.
• Basic understanding of digital marketing practices and tools.
Educational Qualification: Bachelor’s degree in English, Journalism, Communications,
Marketing, or a related field.
Experience: 2-3 Years of experience required
Location: Rajkot, Gujarat
Working Mode: Work from Office (WFO) only
